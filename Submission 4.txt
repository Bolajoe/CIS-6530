Submission focus:
 
Preprocess OpCodes; run SVM, KNN (k=3.5), Decision Tree on 1-gram and 2-gram OpCodes; report metrics including Accuracy, Recall, Precision, F-measure, Confusion

Criteria and weights (100 points)

1.Preprocessing pipeline quality and correctness (20 points)

•A+/A:
 
Preprocessing is clearly justified and consistent; produces stable 1-gram and 2-gram feature representations; addresses leakage risks and class balance considerations.

2.Algorithm implementation fidelity (SVM, KNN, Decision Tree) (20 points)

•A+/A:
 
Correct implementations with sensible parameter handling; clearly separates 1-gram vs 2-gram experiments; multi-class handling is explicit and correct.

3.Experimental design and evaluation methodology (20 points)

•A+/A:

Sound train/test (or cross-validation) procedure; avoids contamination; reports
dataset splits, random seeds, and any tuning strategy.

4.Metrics computation and reporting quality (20 points)

•A+/A:
 
Accurately reports Accuracy, Recall, Precision, F-measure, and Confusion
Matrix; interprets them meaningfully, including multi-class implications and error
patterns.

5.Reproducibility and artifact completeness (15 points)

•A+/A:
 
Includes preprocessing scripts, classifier scripts (with plotting), and processed
dataset folder; clear run instructions; outputs reproducible.

6.Technical report quality (5 points)

•A+/A:
 
Concise, technical, and well-structured; ties results to methodology and
limitations.

Overall band anchors (Submission 4)

•A+ (90–100):
 
Methodologically rigorous multi-class study with clean preprocessing,
correct models, and insightful error analysis.
